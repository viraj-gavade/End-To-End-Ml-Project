Understanding the FWI Prediction System

Our model uses various environmental parameters to predict the Forest Fire Weather Index.

Temperature & Humidity

The model analyzes temperature and relative humidity which are key factors in determining fire risk.

Wind Speed & Rainfall

Wind speed accelerates fire spread while rainfall helps reduce the risk. Both are important predictors.

Advanced Metrics

FFMC, DMC, and ISI are specialized fire weather indices that our model uses to make accurate predictions.

Machine Learning Approach
